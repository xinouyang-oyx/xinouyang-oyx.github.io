Xin Ouyang

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Economics, Tsinghua University and has been a visiting scholar at the Center for Labor and a Just Economy, Harvard University.

My research focuses on technological innovation and identity-based inequality. Methodologically, I primarily use large, unique datasets for causal inference, supplemented by randomized controlled experiments and structural modeling.

Research Fields: Strategy Innovation; Organization Theory; Development Economics; Economic History
